NDT

Through long time industry partnerships, Just Plane Maintenance can handle and oversee your Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) needs, providing a wide range of advanced techniques to ensure the safety and integrity of your aircraft.  NDT methods include, but are not limited to:

  • Eddy Current: Surface scans, bolt hole inspections, conductivity measurements, nonconductive coating measurement, and Weldscan® for precise analysis of metallic surfaces.
  • Ultrasonic: Longitudinal and shear wave flaw detection, as well as thickness gauging, to accurately assess internal structures without compromising material integrity.
  • Magnetic Particle: Wet, fluorescent, horizontal FWDC bench, and portable wet or dry yoke inspections to detect surface and near-surface flaws in ferromagnetic materials.
  • Liquid Penetrant: Fluorescent and visible penetrant testing using Method “A” and “C” removal techniques to detect surface-breaking defects.
  • Composite Material Bond Testing: Advanced techniques for testing the integrity of composite material bonds and identifying core damage, ensuring the strength and durability of non-metallic structures.

Just Plane Maintenance, utilizes highly skilled technicians to perform these NDT techniques to provide thorough, accurate, and efficient assessments, ensuring your aircraft meets the highest standards of safety and performance. Whether you're assessing metal or composite materials, we offer the expertise and tools needed to keep your fleet in optimal condition.
